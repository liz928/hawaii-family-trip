Adalyn's Slushie Bar

ICEMAN 64 oz Slushie Machine

🧊 How to Use the Machine

  1. Pour your mix into the top container — stop at the max fill line (~48 oz). It expands as it churns.
  2. Tap ⏻ Power to wake it up.
  3. Tap 🥤 Slushy mode (or 🍹 Cocktail works too).
  4. Set Thickness with ➖ ➕ — start in the middle. More bars = thicker/icier.
  5. Tap ▶️ Play to start.
  6. Wait ~30–60 min until it's slushy.
  7. Dispense from the front spout. Enjoy! 🥳
  8. After: run Defrost / Clean, then wash the parts.
⚠️ The #1 rule: the mix needs enough sugar to turn slushy. Too little sugar = it freezes into a solid brick and the motor struggles. Don't make it "too healthy" — the sweetness helps it work!

🍍 Virgin Piña Colada

The first one! Dairy-Free ✓

Full batch (64 oz machine)

  • 3 cups pineapple juice (100%)
  • 1½ cups canned coconut milk (full-fat)
  • ⅔ cup cream of coconut (Coco López) — the slush-maker!
  • 3–4 Tbsp sugar or simple syrup (only if juice is tart)
  • Optional: a squeeze of lime

Blend smooth → pour in → Slushy mode → medium thickness → ~30–60 min. Makes 6–8 kid cups.

🥥 Naturally dairy-free for Adalyn — coconut milk + cream of coconut, no dairy. (Coco López is DF — just check the label.)

🎯 Perfect-It Cheat Sheet

  • Freezing too solid / not moving? → needs more sugar. Add cream of coconut or simple syrup.
  • Too watery / won't thicken? → more cream of coconut, less juice. Raise thickness a notch.
  • Too icy? → lower the thickness setting next batch.
  • Not creamy enough? → more coconut milk.
  • Too sweet? → more pineapple juice + a squeeze of lime.
💡 First batch: make it slightly sweeter than you think. Under-sweet is what makes the machine struggle. Once you nail it, write down your ratio!

♻️ Leftover Slush?

  • Fridge (easiest): pour into a jar, keeps 2–3 days. It melts to liquid — just pour back in + re-churn. Shake first if it separated.
  • Freeze: pour into a container or ice cube trays. Thaw → re-churn, OR blend the frozen cubes for instant slush.

Nothing wasted — the mix is forgiving. 🌴

🌈 More Flavors to Try

Strawberry Dairy-Free ✓

🍓 Strawberry Slush

  • 3 cups lemon-lime soda OR white grape juice
  • 2 cups frozen strawberries, thawed + mashed (or 1½ cups strawberry purée)
  • 3–4 Tbsp sugar or simple syrup
  • Squeeze of lemon
Mango Dairy-Free ✓

🥭 Mango Slush

  • 2 cups mango nectar/juice
  • 1½ cups mango purée (blended frozen mango)
  • 1 cup pineapple or orange juice
  • 3 Tbsp sugar or simple syrup + squeeze of lime
Blue Raspberry Dairy-Free ✓

💙 Blue Raspberry Slush

  • 4½ cups blue raspberry lemonade or juice
  • 3–4 Tbsp sugar (if not already sweet)
  • Squeeze of lemon to balance

Classic snow-cone vibes — kids love the color. 💙

Cherry Limeade Dairy-Free ✓

🍒 Cherry Limeade Slush

  • 3 cups limeade
  • 1½ cups cherry juice
  • 2–3 Tbsp sugar + fresh lime squeeze
🧊 All flavors: aim for ~48 oz total liquid, keep them on the sweeter side so they slush, pour to the max line, run Slushy mode ~30–60 min.
