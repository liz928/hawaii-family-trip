villas + kitchen

🏨 Timbers Kauai

📍 Hokuala / Lihue (east) · ~5 min to airport
luxury · closest to activities
  • 3-bed signature residences (everyone own room)
  • Closest to airport + Wailua activities (luau, tubing, chocolate)
  • Oceanfront golf resort, Hualani's restaurant
  • Priciest, but central to your east-side activity cluster
💡 Most of your activities cluster on the EAST side (Lihue/Wailua: air tour, luau, tubing, chocolate, fern grotto) + SOUTH (Poipu beaches). Catamaran is southwest (Port Allen), Waimea Canyon is far west.
Poipu lodging (Grand Hyatt/Koloa) = ~25 min to airport but best beaches + sun. Lihue lodging (Timbers) = closest to most activities but less "beach resort" feel.
