🏝️ The Two Islands - Different Vibes

🟢 KAUAI - "The Garden Isle" (Days 1-8)

Lush, dramatic, slower pace. Movie-set scenery (Jurassic Park, Avatar, Pirates of the Caribbean were filmed here). Best for adventure + waterfalls. South shore (Poipu) = sunny + calm beaches.

🌊 Beaches
Poipu Beach (calm, family-friendly), Hanalei Bay (north shore beauty)
🏞️ Landscapes
Na Pali Coast cliffs, Waimea Canyon "Grand Canyon of Pacific"
🛶 Best for
Kayaking, snorkeling, helicopter tours, waterfall hikes
🌧️ Weather
July = warm + sunny in Poipu (south); occasional north shore showers
🍽️ Food scene
Farm-to-table, fresh fish, Hanalei tropical eats, James Beard chefs
⏰ Pace
Slow, intimate, fewer tourists, "old Hawaii" vibe

🟠 BIG ISLAND - "Hawaii's Wild Side" (Days 9-15)

Vast, geological, raw. Active volcano (Kīlauea is erupting NOW), world's tallest mountain (Mauna Kea from base), 8 of 13 climate zones. Best for adventure + variety.

🌋 Adventure
Active volcano, lava glow at night, walk crater floor
🌌 Stargazing
Mauna Kea = #1 stargazing on Earth, 9,200ft visitor center FREE
🐟 Marine life
Manta ray night snorkel (96% success rate!), sea turtles, coral reefs
🏖️ Beaches
White, black, green sand beaches (Punaluʻu, Mahana, Hapuna)
☕ Food + Culture
Kona coffee farms, vanilla farm, cacao, Loco Moco birthplace (Hilo)
⏰ Pace
Spread out, lots of driving between regions, more variety
