👜 Ball Arena Bag Policy

Strict!

Wallet-size bags only

Ball Arena has one of the strictest bag limits around. Allowed: a small clutch, wristlet, or fanny pack no bigger than 4" x 6" x 1.5" (about wallet-size). Doesn't have to be clear — just tiny.

✅ Plan: everyone carries a small wallet/wristlet or a tiny fanny pack. Phone + card + ID + lip gloss. That's it. Bigger bags get turned away at the door.
🍼 Diaper/medical bags allowed up to 14x14x6 via VIP/ADA Door #1.

Upscale mall (Lululemon, teen brands, everything) PLUS the walkable Cherry Creek North streets — boutiques, cafés, murals. Matcha + Mango Mango are close by, so Wednesday can be one easy cluster: shop → matcha → mango → repeat.
